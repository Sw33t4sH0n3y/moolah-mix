# 💰 Moolah M$X
[Moolah M$x](https://moolah-mix-5fc7bead314b.herokuapp.com/)

**Stack Your Splits. Secure Your Bag.**

> *The reimagined evolution of [Payout-Pitch](https://payoutpitch-8fa758c596dd.herokuapp.com/) — rebuilt with a fresh identity, enhanced features.*

A split sheet management app for musicians, producers, and songwriters who want to protect their work *before* the check clears.

---

## 🎯 The Problem

You made a hit, now everyone wants a piece. But who owns what?

Split sheets get lost. Handshake deals are forgotten. When the royalties roll in, chaos.

---

## 💡 The Solution

**Moolah M$X** documents ownership, manages collaborators, and generates professional split sheets — all in one place.

---

## ✨ Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| 🔐 **User Auth** | Secure sign-up/sign-in with password reset |
| 🎵 **Track Management** | Add, edit, delete your songs |
| 👥 **Multiple Artists** | Primary, featured, remix, producer roles |
| 🤝 **Collaborators** | Add contributors with full role details |
| 📊 **Split Sheets** | Master + Publishing splits that lock at 100% |
| ✍️ **E-Signatures** | Draw or type signatures for agreements |
| 📧 **Email Invites** | Send collaborators invite links to review & sign |
| 📄 **PDF Export** | Download professional split sheets with signatures |
| 🔊 **SoundCloud Search** | Find tracks and auto-fill details |
| 💰 **Revenue Calculator** | Estimate earnings across streaming platforms |
| 🌙 **Dark/Light Mode** | Easy on the eyes |

| **Email** | Nodemailer |
